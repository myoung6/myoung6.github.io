Directions

Here is a quick 7 step guide to make some delicious fried rice - Japanese steak house style! If you like fried rice as much as I do or want to cook for some friends or family, I recommend doubling or even tripling the ingredients to make more!

  1. Prepare white rice using instructions on packaging, let cool and set aside.

  2. Meanwhile, chop all vegetables into finely chopped pieces, set aside in prep bowls.

  3. In a large skillet or wok, bring oil to just under high heat.

  4. Fry one egg until no longer runny and break into small pieces using the tip of a spatula.

  5. Add vegetables to the wok and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add rice and soy sauce.

  6. OPTIONAL: add in yum yum sauce and stir in. Can wait til after done cooking to add it seperately.

  7. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes. Serve immediately.
